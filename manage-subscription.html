Subscription

All subscriptions are processed securely via PayPal. You can cancel anytime.

🛡️
Important — Cancellation Deadline You must cancel at least 24 hours before your next billing date to avoid being charged for the next cycle. No partial refunds are issued for unused periods.
🔵 Cancel via PayPal Account

If you subscribed using your PayPal account, follow these steps:

1
Log in at paypal.com
2
Click your profile icon → Settings (gear icon)
3
Click Payments in the top menu
4
Select "Manage automatic payments"
5
Find VivaSculpt Studio in the list
6
Click Cancel → confirm cancellation
→ Go directly to PayPal Automatic Payments
💳 Paid by Card Without a PayPal Account?

If you paid as a guest with a card, contact PayPal support directly to cancel.

1
Go to paypal.com/smarthelp/home
2
Click "Contact Us" or start a live chat
3
Say: "I want to cancel a billing agreement for VivaSculpt Studio"
4
Provide the email address you used at checkout
→ Go to PayPal Support
⚠️
After cancellation Your access continues until the end of your current billing period. After that date, you will lose access to VivaSculpt Studio. You can resubscribe at any time.

❓ Billing FAQ
When will I be charged?
Starter: after your 7-day free trial ends, then every 30 days. Pro: immediately upon subscribing, then every 30 days.
Can I get a refund?
We don't offer refunds for partial billing periods unless required by your local consumer protection law. Contact us within 48 hours of an unexpected charge and we'll do our best to help.
Can I switch from Starter to Pro?
Yes — cancel your Starter plan and subscribe to Pro. Your Starter access continues until the end of the current period.
What happens to my data if I cancel?
Your cycle tracking data and progress are stored locally in your browser. Cancelling your subscription does not delete this data — it remains on your device.
I cancelled but was still charged. What do I do?
Email hello@vivasculptstudioapp.com with your PayPal transaction ID and we'll resolve it within 24 hours.
